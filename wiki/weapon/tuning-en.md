# Tuning

---

## Tuning Concept

### Basic Concept
The act of replacing parts of the weapon's base body to apply desired characteristics.

Replaceable parts:
- **Barrel**
- **Receiver** (body)
- **Stock** -- standard pistols and some SMGs use attachments instead
- **Pistol grip**
- **Skin** (appearance and paint scheme)

---

## Part Management

- Parts can be stored in bags or pouches
- Assembled weapons cannot be stored in bags/pouches

### Weapon Disassembly
Disassembling a weapon breaks it into base components, each stored separately in the bag:
- Barrel
- Handguard
- Receiver (upper+lower integrated)
- Stock (except pistols)

---

## Tuning Parts by Function

### Barrel

| Part | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Rifled barrel** | Decreased close-range kinetic energy, increased effective range |
| **Smoothbore barrel** | Increased close-range kinetic energy, decreased effective range |
| **Precision barrel** (sniper rifles) | Reduced MOA value |
| **Heavy barrel** (LMG exclusive) | Recoil decreases as sustained fire increases |

### Handguard
- Part dependent on the barrel
- Provides under rail and side rail attachment slots
- Some SMGs have rails integrated into the receiver with no separate handguard

### Receiver

| Part | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **High-pressure gas control device** | Increased rate of fire, decreased grouping stability |
| **Fire rate profile change** | Increased initial rate of fire, decreased sustained rate of fire |
| **Fire mode change** | Toggle between full-auto and semi-auto/burst |

### Pistol Grip

| Part | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Lightweight grip** | Increased weapon swap speed, increased muzzle sway while ADS |
| **Heavy grip** | Decreased weapon sway while ADS, decreased ADS speed |
| **Tactical grip** | Increased ADS speed from sprint |

### Stock

| Part | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Support stock** | High ADS stability, heavy |
| **Lightweight stock** | Increased weapon swap/ADS speed, decreased ADS stability |
| **Buffer stock** | Reduced recoil, increased MOA |
