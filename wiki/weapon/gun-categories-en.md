# Weapon Categories

---

## Summary Comparison Table

| Category | Effective Range | Mobility | Rate of Fire | Characteristics |
|----------|----------------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| Assault Rifle | Medium | Average | Average | Hexagonal stat profile, versatility |
| Submachine Gun | Close | High | Very High | Mobile strikes, fast reaction |
| Sniper Rifle | Long | Low | Very Low | Precision sniping, high damage |
| Light Machine Gun | Medium | Very Low | High | Firepower projection, suppressive fire |
| Shotgun | Point-blank | Average | Low | One-shot-kill clearing |

---

## 1. Assault Rifle (AR)

**Design goal: The symbol of 'versatility' -- capable of handling any situation.**

- **All-rounder**: Multi-purpose weapon adaptable from close to medium-long range
- **Solid stopping power**: Well-balanced between rate of fire and damage for stable DPS
- **Reliable accuracy**: High first-shot accuracy; precise hits achievable in burst or semi-auto fire
- **Standard mobility**: Movement speed and ADS speed serve as the baseline for all weapon categories
- **Rich modularity**: Best suited for customization with optics, vertical grips, and various attachments

---

## 2. Submachine Gun (SMG)

**Design goal: 'Close-quarters combat and mobile strikes' through speed and rate of fire.**

- **High rate of fire**: More bullets per unit time, overwhelming burst damage in close quarters
- **Top-tier mobility**: Lightweight, fastest movement speed and most responsive ADS
- **Hip-fire specialist**: Tight hip-fire spread makes it advantageous in chaotic firefights
- **Low recoil control**: Small caliber makes recoil easy to manage, but damage drops sharply at range
- **Indoor king**: First to breach defensive lines in tight interiors and around corners

---

## 3. Sniper Rifle (SR)

**Design goal: 'Long-range precision shooting and observation' -- eliminating targets from unseen positions.**

- **Extreme one-shot power**: Neutralize targets with one headshot or 1-2 body shots
- **Maximum range**: One-sided engagement possible at distances no other weapon can reach
- **Low rate of fire/mobility**: Slow rechamber (bolt action, etc.) and very sluggish ADS/movement speed
- **Vulnerable when exposed**: Extremely difficult to counter close-range enemies; high risk of missing a shot
- **Intelligence gathering**: Can use high-magnification scopes to identify enemy positions and brief allies -- reconnaissance capability

---

## 4. Light Machine Gun (LMG)

**Design goal: 'Area suppression and fire support' backed by overwhelming magazine capacity.**

- **Overwhelming sustain**: 50-100+ round high-capacity magazines enable prolonged fire without reloading
- **Suppression effect**: Continuous fire keeps enemies' heads down, enabling ally movement
- **High penetration**: Can penetrate thin cover to hit hiding enemies
- **Heavy penalty**: Slowest movement speed; very long reload time leaves you defenseless
- **Deployed fire recommended**: High recoil means performance is maximized in defensive positions, prone or deployed

---

## 5. Shotgun (SG)

**Design goal: 'Guaranteed one-shot kill' at point-blank range.**

- **Overwhelming stopping power**: Explosive firepower capable of instant kills with a single shot within effective range
- **Spread attack**: Multiple projectiles per shot enable effective hits even without precise aim
- **Psychological pressure**: The mere knowledge that a shotgun user is in a tight space deters enemy entry
- **Range limitation**: Damage drops to near-zero just meters outside effective range -- range management is directly tied to survival
