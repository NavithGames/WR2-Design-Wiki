# Delta Force Gunplay Reference Video List

---

## 1. Gameplay Videos

### 1-1. Extraction Mode Gameplay
**Delta Force Hawk Ops Extraction : RTX 4090 24GB ( 4K Maximum Settings RTX ON / DLSS ON )**

For checking gunplay flow in the extraction genre context. In situations with low engagement density and lots of movement, you can observe gunplay patterns in real combat tempo: hip fire to ADS transitions, weapon holding posture during movement, post-engagement return posture, etc. This is the play environment most similar to the PvPvE extraction context WR2 is targeting.

### 1-2. Conquest Mode Infantry Gameplay
**Tactical Aiming is weirdly good - Delta Force Multiplayer [No Commentary]**

For checking gunplay in a high-engagement-frequency PvP environment. Tactical Stance (laser aiming posture) usage appears frequently; you can closely observe sprint-to-instant-ADS, moving fire, and stance transition responsiveness. Suitable for understanding the practical application flow of the Hip / Tac Stance / ADS 3-stage stance system.

### 1-3. Conquest Mode Vehicle Gameplay
**Delta Force: Hill of Iron gameplay (No Commentary)**

Hill of Iron is a Warfare sub-mode focused on heavy vehicles like tanks and APCs. This video captures 4K 60fps no-commentary tank combat gameplay on Map: Cracked, allowing the clearest observation of vehicle-mounted movement/firing/damage response and other vehicle combat flow. Used as reference when designing WR2's vehicle-based PvPvE engagement structures.

## 2. Animation Videos

### 2-1. Weapon Inspection Animation Collection
**Delta Force - All Weapons Inspect Animations**

Full weapon inspection animation collection. You can observe the holding posture in idle state while equipped, grip position differences per weapon, and inspection animation start/end poses. Useful for comparing base holding posture differences across weapon categories (pistol / SMG / rifle / sniper / LMG).

### 2-2. Weapon Reload Animation Slow Motion
**Delta Force: Hawk Ops - All Weapon Reload Animations In Slow Motion**

Full weapon reload animation in slow motion. Allows frame-by-frame observation of hand positions, weapon tilt angles, and posture recovery flow at reload start/completion that are hard to see at normal speed. The recovery motion returning to firing posture after reload and the weight expression method are particularly visible.

### 2-3. Delta Force vs COD Animation Comparison
**Delta Force Copied COD Entirely Action Movement Comparison**

A video directly comparing movement/gunplay animations between Delta Force and Call of Duty. By placing sprint cancel speed, ADS transition timing, and weapon sway handling during movement side-by-side, the structural characteristics of Delta Force's animations become clearly apparent. Can be referenced for establishing our game's stance transition response speed and weight feel standards.

### 2-4. Delta Force Full Weapon Animation Showcase
**DELTA FORCE : ALL WEAPON WITH ATTACHMENT SHOWCASE [SEASON 1-8] 4K**

A comprehensive 52-minute showcase covering all weapons from seasons 1-8 with attachments equipped. Organized by category with timestamps: pistol / SMG / shotgun / assault rifle / LMG / marksman / sniper. You can compare weapon silhouette changes with attachments, reload flows, and holding postures by category, making it suitable as a per-category base posture reference.
